About the fest:

Athlos is a Greek word of contest. It is said that excitement is impossible where there is no contest. To maintain the excitement to compete and enhance the sportsmanship of the players, we thereby present the name of our sports fest - Athlos. The university is built among the divine beauty of hills. From the last years it is setting a great example among academics as well as sports. Every day is a new day. You can build your life on yesterday’s success or put its failures behind and start over again. You can’t win unless you know how to lose. Adversity causes some people to break, others to break records. Don’t look back. Something might be gaining on you. So just play, have fun and enjoy the life.

7-A Side Soccer

The sport of soccer is considered to be the world’s most popular sport. In soccer there are two teams of seven players. Soccer is played on a large grass field with a goal at each end. The object of the game is to get the soccer ball into the opposing team's goal. The key to soccer is that, with the exception of the goalie, players cannot touch the ball with their hands, they can only kick, knee, or head the ball to advance it or score a goal.
